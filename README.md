## 🔌 Connecting your device 🔌

There are two ways to connect your Android device.

### USB connection

1. Use your USB cable to plug your device into your computer.
2. Unlock your phone screen.
3. Look for a notification on your phone about USB settings.
4. Set the USB mode to File Transfer or MTP mode.
5. Wait for the app to recognize your device. The device name will appear in the main window.

### Wi-Fi connection

1. Ensure your phone and computer share the same network.
2. Enable Developer Options on your Android device.
3. Turn on USB Debugging within the Developer Options menu.
4. Use the pairing option in the app to sync the devices over the network.
5. The device will be ready for use once the connection light turns green.

## 📂 Managing your files 📂

The app displays your device storage in a simple list. You interact with this list just like your standard file explorer.

### Moving files to the computer

1. Select the files or folders you want to copy.
2. Right-click the selected items and choose Download.
3. Pick a folder on your computer to save the files.
4. Keep the devices connected until the progress bar reaches the end.

### Moving files to the device

1. Select the files on your computer.
2. Drag and drop the files into the device window.
3. Click Confirm if a prompt appears on your phone screen.
4. Wait for the indicator to signal that the transfer is complete.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting common issues 🛠️

If you face trouble, try these steps to fix the connection.

- Use a different USB port on your computer.
- Use an original manufacturer cable for the best results.
- Ensure your phone is not in Power Saving mode.
- Update your Android system software if the device fails to show up.
- Restart both your phone and your computer, then try the connection again.

The app uses common communication standards. It works with most modern Android phones and tablets. If you use a specific older device, you might need to install additional manufacturer drivers from the device website.

## 🛡️ Privacy and security 🛡️

Your data stays local to your machine. The app does not send your files or personal data to any remote servers. All file operations happen directly between the connected hardware and your internal storage.
